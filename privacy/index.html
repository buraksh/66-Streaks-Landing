Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2025

66-Day Streak ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles information. By using 66-Day Streak, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. 66-Day Streak is designed to operate entirely offline and locally on your device.

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Habit names and icons you create
  • Streak progress and check-in dates
  • Reminder times and notification preferences
  • App appearance settings (theme preference)

This data is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's local storage frameworks (SwiftData). We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure. Your data remains on your device at all times.

3. No Account Required

66-Day Streak does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information to use the App. There is no login, registration, or authentication process.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or third-party libraries that collect user data. We do not track your behavior, usage patterns, or any other activity within the App.

5. Notifications

66-Day Streak may send local notifications to remind you about your habits. These notifications are:

  • Generated and scheduled entirely on your device
  • Never processed by external servers
  • Fully customizable and can be disabled in your device settings

6. Third-Party Services

66-Day Streak does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs. The App functions entirely independently and does not communicate with any external systems.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third party. Since all data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it, there is no data available for us to share.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Erase All Data" option in the App's Settings
  • Deleting the App from your device (this permanently removes all locally stored data)

9. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and biometric locks. Since your data never leaves your device and is never transmitted over the internet, it is not subject to network-based security risks.

10. Children's Privacy

66-Day Streak does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect any personal information from any user, there is no risk of children's data being collected or processed.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
